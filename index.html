Challenges Teens Face

Modern teens are battling anxiety, depression, cyberbullying, social pressures, and academic stress. Awareness and support are critical.

How to Support Teens

Encouraging open communication, offering professional help, promoting wellness activities, and building trust are key to supporting teen mental health.
